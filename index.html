About Charlie Rao, MS, LPC-A

Hi, I’m Charlie Rao, a Licensed Professional Counselor Associate based in Missouri City, TX. I specialize in helping adults, teens, and families—especially those from multicultural or South Asian backgrounds—navigate anxiety, depression, and bipolar disorder with warmth, understanding, and culturally informed care.

Services Offered

  • Individual Therapy for adults, teens, and seniors
  • Support for Anxiety, Depression, and Bipolar Disorder
  • Culturally Competent Therapy for South Asian families and third-culture individuals
  • Support for Caregivers & Multigenerational Households

In-person & Virtual Telehealth Sessions Available
