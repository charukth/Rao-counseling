FAQs

What is therapy?

Therapy is a supportive process to explore thoughts, feelings, and behaviors for growth and healing.

What is counseling?

Counseling often focuses on specific issues or goals with a solution-oriented approach.

How long will therapy last?

It varies — some clients meet short-term, others benefit from ongoing support. We’ll decide together.

What is your fee rate?

$150 for initial 90-min session, $100 for 60-min follow-ups. Sliding scale available.

What if I feel stuck or not making progress?

We’ll check in, revisit goals, and adapt our approach. Therapy is a collaborative space.

Is therapy confidential?

Yes, with few legal exceptions (like safety concerns). Your privacy matters deeply.

Can I contact you between sessions?

Yes — for scheduling or quick check-ins via the secure message portal.
