Frequently Asked Questions

What is therapy?

Therapy is a process of exploring emotions and behaviors with a trained professional to improve mental health and well-being.

What is counseling?

Counseling is a supportive process that helps individuals overcome challenges, make decisions, or improve relationships through conversation.

How long will therapy last?

Therapy length varies; it could range from a few sessions to long-term depending on individual needs and goals.

What is your fee rate?

Initial session: $150 for 90 minutes. Ongoing sessions: $100 for 1 hour. Sliding scale is available.

What if I feel stuck or not making progress?

It's important to discuss these feelings with your therapist to adjust goals or methods to better fit your needs.

Is therapy confidential?

Yes, therapy is confidential, with some exceptions like risk of harm to yourself or others.

Can I contact you between sessions?

You can contact me for brief check-ins or urgent matters, but it's important to respect boundaries outside of scheduled sessions.

How many sessions will I need?

The number of sessions depends on your goals and progress. We can assess together as we go along.
